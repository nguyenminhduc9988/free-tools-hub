Free Text to Speech — Read Any Text Aloud

This free text-to-speech tool uses your browser's built-in Speech Synthesis API. No API keys, no sign-up, no data sent to servers — it works 100% offline once the page loads. Great for proofreading essays, listening to articles, learning pronunciation, or accessibility support.

Available voices depend on your operating system and browser. Most modern browsers offer multiple English voices plus other languages. Adjust rate for faster/slower playback and pitch for higher/lower tone.
