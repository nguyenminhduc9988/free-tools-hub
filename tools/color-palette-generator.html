Color Theory — Building Better Palettes

This palette generator creates harmonious color schemes using proven color theory: complementary colors sit opposite each other on the wheel (high contrast), analogous colors sit next to each other (calm, cohesive), and triadic colors form an equilateral triangle (vibrant balance).
