How Accurate Is This Speed Test?

This speed test measures real-world performance by downloading and uploading payloads from Cloudflare's edge network. Results depend on your ISP, distance to the server, wifi signal, and concurrent network activity. For best accuracy: connect via ethernet, close other downloads/uploads, and pause streaming.

Typical home internet speeds: DSL 5-25 Mbps, cable 50-500 Mbps, fiber 500-2000 Mbps. Mobile 4G averages 20-50 Mbps; 5G can hit 200-1000 Mbps in good coverage.
